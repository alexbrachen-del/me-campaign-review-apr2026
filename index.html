4 Insights & Recommendations

For Marketing

1. "Fee High" = Value Communication Gap

30% of March non-paid TDs objected to price. Parents do trials, like the product, then balk at cost. Test creatives that anchor price upfront — set 12-month value expectations, filter low-budget parents via lead forms.

2. High_ROAS_Creative is a Volume Trap

8% T2P in March, 17% in April. #1 QL source but attracts browsers. Cap budget. Shift incremental spend to Interest_Maths_Engaged (43% T2P — best Meta campaign).

3. Google Generic KWs = Worst Full-Funnel

18% QL-TD% AND 12% T2P. Only 1 payment from 24 March QLs. Generic searchers comparison shop and find Cuemath expensive. Cap or reduce budget.

4. Grade 0-1 Targeting Wastes Trial Capacity

65 March QLs, 24 TDs, only 2 payments (8% T2P). "Child Too Young" is frequent. Add Grade 2-8 filter — every Grade 0 trial slot = lost Grade 2-4 slot (35-41% T2P).

5. Advantage+Non-OTP Starved Again

40% QL-TD% in March but only ₹0.8L spent (₹2.5L planned). April: 1 QL in 10 days. Either commit budget or remove from plan.

6. Budget Deployment Discipline

₹7L never deployed in March (3 campaigns never launched). Every campaign must launch by Day 3 or budget reallocated by Day 5. Weekly pacing review non-negotiable.

For Sales

1. Fix "Decision Pending" — Create Urgency

35% of April non-paid TDs are stalling (was 8% in March). They didn't say no — they haven't decided. Every post-trial call needs an expiring offer: "Slot available Monday. Confirm by Thursday or we offer to another family."

2. "Fee High" Handling Framework

34 leads lost to fee objection (28 Mar + 6 Apr). ACs need scripted response: (a) per-session reframe, (b) 12-month savings, (c) EMI options, (d) KHDA exam urgency anchor.

3. Log Disposition Reasons — Always

24% of March non-paid TDs have no reason logged. Can't diagnose what we can't measure. Every NI lead must have a reason same day.

4. Grade-Specific Pitches

Grade 5: "Build foundation before Grade 6-7 gets harder." Grade 8: "4 weeks to board exams — last window." Both have <10% T2P with Fee High dominant.
